Super STEM Saturday

A FREE, FUN, INFORMATIVE, STEM-TASTIC EVENT!

October 15, 1 - 4pm
Columbus State, Mitchell Hall
250 Cleveland Ave., Columbus

Students will enjoy hands-on activities, games and simulations that demonstrate high paying careers like materials science, construction, user experience testing, engineering, and more. You will take home cool stuff just for attending!

Parents can learn about educational pathways to fulfilling careers and pick up tips on how to guide your children toward success.
